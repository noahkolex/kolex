Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Kolex is a Chrome extension that replaces the AI loading spinner with a single sponsored line — and pays you for it. We built it privacy-first on purpose, because the whole thing only works if you trust it. This page explains, in plain English, exactly what we do and do not collect.

The short version

Kolex never reads your prompts, your conversations, your files, or your browsing history. None of that ever leaves your device. The extension only checks a few CSS selectors to know one thing: is the model still working? The only data we send is an anonymous device id plus impression and click counts — and we don't send anything at all until you opt in.

What we collect

We keep this list deliberately tiny.

Anonymous device id

A random identifier generated on your device so we can credit your earnings to the right browser. It is not tied to your name, and it isn't an advertising id.

Impression & click counts

When a sponsored line is shown (an impression) or clicked, we count it. That's how advertisers are billed and how you get paid. We count the event — we do not record what you were doing at the time.

Portal account

If you create an account to cash out, we store your email and a securely hashed password. That's it. Your password is never stored in plain text.

Advertiser billing

Advertiser payments are handled by Stripe. We never see or store card numbers — Stripe processes them directly.

What we do NOT collect

This is the part that matters most, so we'll be blunt. Kolex does not collect, read, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your prompts or anything you type into an AI tool
  • Your conversations or the model's responses
  • The contents of any page you visit
  • Your browsing history or the URLs you open
  • Files, documents, or attachments on your device

No page content ever leaves your device. There is no hidden upload, no analytics on your activity, and no profile built from what you read or write.

How the extension works technically

Kolex doesn't need to understand your content to do its job — it only needs to know whether the AI is currently generating a response. To detect that, it queries a small set of CSS selectors (the same kind a stylesheet uses) to see if the page is showing its "busy" or "thinking" state. That's the full extent of what it inspects: a yes/no answer to "is the model working?"

When the model is busy, Kolex swaps the spinner for a sponsored line. Ad creatives are delivered as inline data URLs, which means the extension makes no outbound network requests to render an ad — so nothing about what you're looking at could be inferred from a request to a third party.

Cookies & local storage

We use a session token to keep you logged in to the earnings portal, and we store your local settings (such as whether Kolex is turned on) on your device. We don't use tracking cookies and we don't share this with advertisers.

Payments

Payments — both advertiser billing and your payouts — are processed by Stripe. Card details are handled entirely by Stripe under their own security and privacy practices; Kolex never stores them. Your use of payments is also subject to Stripe's privacy policy.

Third parties

We keep third parties to a minimum:

  • Stripe — processes advertiser billing and your payouts.
  • The advertiser's destination — if you choose to click a sponsored line, your browser navigates to that advertiser's site, just like clicking any other link. We don't share your identity with them.

Your choices

You're in control at every step:

  • Consent toggle — Kolex is consent-gated. It serves nothing and sends nothing until you opt in, and you can turn it off instantly at any time.
  • Uninstall — remove the extension from Chrome and it stops entirely, immediately.
  • Data deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us at privacy@kolex.ai.

Data retention

We retain impression and click counts and account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide payouts and meet legal and accounting obligations. When you request deletion, we remove your account data, keeping only what we're legally required to retain (such as records of payments already made).

Children

Kolex is not intended for children. You must be at least 13 (or 16 where required by local law) to use it. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under that age.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Kolex evolves. When we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the extension or portal.

Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about your privacy? Email us at privacy@kolex.ai and we'll help.
