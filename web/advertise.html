Questions, answered

Where exactly does my ad show?

On the loading / "thinking…" indicator inside ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok, for users who run the Kolex extension. Your brand and copy replace the spinner itself.

How is pricing set?

You bid in dollars for blocks of 1,000 five-second impressions. It's a live auction — the highest bid serves first. You only ever spend the budget you buy.

How fast does it go live?

Immediately. The moment your Stripe payment clears, your campaign enters the auction and starts serving.

Can I edit or relaunch later?

Yes. Your advertiser portal lets you edit the creative anytime, track impressions and clicks, and relaunch a finished campaign with a fresh budget in one click.
