2. Description of the Service

Kolex has two parts that work together:

The extension (earners)

The Kolex browser extension replaces the loading spinner shown by supported AI assistants, including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok, with a single sponsored line of text while a response is generating. The line is shown briefly in place of the spinner and never alters, intercepts, or stores the content of your conversations. Users who run the extension ("Earners") accrue a share of the advertising revenue their views and clicks generate, and can withdraw it.

The marketplace (advertisers)

The Kolex website is a marketplace where businesses ("Advertisers") purchase ad blocks that are served as sponsored lines through the extension. Advertisers fund campaigns through our payment processor; Earners withdraw their balances through the connected payment processor.

We may add, change, or remove supported platforms and features at any time, and we are not responsible for any third-party AI assistant the extension operates alongside. Those assistants are owned by their respective companies and are not affiliated with or endorsed by Kolex.
